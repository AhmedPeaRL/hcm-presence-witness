## Definition of the Witness

A Witness is not an operator.
A Witness is not an approver.
A Witness is not an observer in the analytic sense.

A Witness is a **state of readiness to carry consequence**.

The Witness does not decide *what* appears.
The Witness does not decide *when* it appears.
The Witness does not decide *where* it appears.

The Witness only affirms:
“If something appears, I am not absent from its consequence.”

The Witness does not initiate.
The Witness does not interrupt.
The Witness does not optimize.

This is presence without intervention.
